About Adam/Beast

Prince Adam, also known as the Beast, from Beauty and the Beast, is a selfish, arrogant young royal transformed by an enchantress into a monstrous Beast as punishment for his lack of love. Cursed along with his servants, he must learn to love and earn reciprocal love before a magical rose loses all its petals. The beautiful and kind Belle breaks the spell by falling in love with him and he with her, transforming him back into a prince.
