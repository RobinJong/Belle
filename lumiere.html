About Lumière

Lumière, from Beauty and the Beast, is the charismatic, kindhearted, and rebellious maître d' of the Beast's castle, transformed into a golden candelabra by the Enchantress's curse. As a central, romantic character, he often clashes with Cogsworth due to his carefree nature. He leads the "Be Our Guest" musical number and plays a key role in fostering romance between Belle and the Beast.
