About Belle

Belle, from Beauty and the Beast, is a bright and independent young woman, who dreams of leaving her quiet provincial life for adventure. To save her father, she agrees to become a prisoner of a fearsome Beast in his enchanted castle. Through compassion and courage, she breaks the spell by falling in love with the Beast, transforming him back into a prince.
